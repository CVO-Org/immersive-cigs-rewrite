## List of Features

### Multiplayer-Syncronised Effects
Using an lighter, or simply puffing a cigarette, your friends (and foes) nearby can hear it.

### Glow in the Night
Puffing a Cigarette will create a faint lightsource which will be visible at night. Same goes for using lighters - but maybe not so faint.
Also, you might blind your NVG-wearing friends.

### Cigarettes on AI
Will Distribute Cigarettes based on CBA Addon settings randomly across all AI units - both, preplaced in mission but also spawned in by zeus or script.

### Dynamic AI Smoking
Units given cigarettes by "Cigs on AI" will dynamically start smoking their cigarettes.

### ACE Advanced Fatigue Support
When ACE Advanced Fatigue is enabled, smoking alot will affect a players stamina. Chainsmokers will start to cough, which can aleart nearby enemies.

### Puff-based Economy
Instead of consuming your beloveth cancer-sticks for a hard-coded duration, each Cigarette has its own "Puff-Budget" which gets reduced by a randomised value with each draw on the cigarette. The randomized value is also being affected by playerside CBA Addon Settings.

Yes - you can become Thomas the Vape-Engine.


### Spontaneous Combustion
Chance to set player on fire if they start smoking in an area where they shouldnt.

### Flavoured Cigarettes and Lollipops
Cigarettes and Lollipops can be configured with a flavour, which will be displayed to the players.
Some rumours say that the Baja also affects the colour of the smoke!

### Dont like smoking? Just eat them
Yes, you can eat cigarettes. Dont ask me why.

### You wish to pay respect to your fallen comrade?
Let them have their last cigarette
